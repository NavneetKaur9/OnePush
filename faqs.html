Is Trading for You?

What’s the best way to learn about Stock Market?

You can read as many articles as you want about the Stock Market, but many traders say that they don’t fully grasp the market until they watch expert traders live and see how successful trading techniques and strategies are applied in the market. We provide a variety of resources to give you that first-hand experience with the market as you begin trading — plus a team of Trading Tycoons experts who will be by your side as you get your feet wet and beyond.

What if I wanted to learn on my own first?

We completely understand people who want to learn on their own first, but we also know how costly the School of Hard Knocks can be. We have been there and done that and we have helped many traders around the globe who tried trading the hard way first, before coming to Trading Tycoons. We can help you when you are ready whether you want proven trading information to work with now or later.

How long will it take me to learn to trade?

The first step is to attend one of our live session, which will provide you with all of the details you’ll need. Once you learn how the Stock market works, and taking advantage of our expert training and technologies, it should take only one to five hours each week to get you on the right track to begin a demo trading account in your first month, and then start live trading as you become comfortable with the market.

What do I do if I want to demo trade before I go live?

We encourages you to practice trading in a demo account before moving into the live market; we even provide our Analyst on Demand broadcast so you can watch an experienced trader analyze and trade the market in real time — insight you could use as you practice placing trades. Demo accounts are a great method for testing your understanding of critical trading techniques and strategies while also giving you experience controlling your emotions while you trade.

Why start now?

There are trading opportunities — with real money-making potential — happening on the Stock market right now and the longer you wait to learn how you could cash in on these market waves, the more money you could be losing. That could be one less paycheck that you could be cashing from your trading wins… The real question to ask yourself is when do you plan on making money? Planning when to begin your training should be solely dependent on when you’d like to start seeing a return on your investment.

Money is tight right now. Do you have options for me?

We have a number of payment options available. Whatever is in your way, we want to help you overcome it — especially if it is a financial concern. We don’t want your financial constraints now to keep you from the education that could help those constraints disappear.

I’m an experienced trader. What can I learn from your sessions?

The Stock market changes every day — and often throughout each day. There’s always more to learn. As Ray Kroc once said, “If you’re green, you’re growing. When you’re ripe, you rot.”

There’s always room to learn new techniques that could help you advance your current skills. We feature new strategies constantly, as well as insight into the successful trader’s psychology, training on avoiding toxic trading mistakes that could ruin your current trades, and even information on the latest trading opportunities on the horizon. Let us show you what you don’t know about the Stock market!
